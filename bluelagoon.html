BLUE LAGOON ISLAND, BAHAMAS

Blue Lagoon Island offers visitors everything one dreams of when picturing a private tropical island - breathtakingly clear turquoise waters including a hidden lagoon, hundreds of native coconut palm trees, pristine white sandy beaches, beautiful natural vegetation, tropical birds, coral reefs, dolphins and sea lions in their natural habitat. Although Blue Lagoon Island is in very close proximity to Nassau and Paradise Island, it is completely secluded allowing all who visit a true opportunity to relax in a tranquil natural environment right on the beach in the Bahamas. The island has remained virtually untouched maintaining its lush natural beauty.
